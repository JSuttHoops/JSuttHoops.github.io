# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

People who want to understand Jacob Sutton's work: basketball readers, collaborators, hiring managers, and people following JSuttHoops projects.

## Product Purpose

This site collects Jacob's portfolio, writing, basketball analysis, and public data projects in one permanent place.

## Brand Personality

Clear, curious, lightly technical. The site should feel personal and edited, not like a startup landing page or a template.

## Anti-references

Avoid AI-generated portfolio tropes: generic hero slogans, decorative numbered scaffolding, fake cards, dark-mode default, overexplained sections, and basketball branding that hides the person behind the work.

## Design Principles

- Lead with Jacob Sutton, with JSuttHoops as context.
- Keep the page direct enough that links and projects are easy to scan.
- Use motion as a quiet surface detail, not as the main event.
- Prefer plain language over slogans.
- Leave room for writing, reading, and non-basketball work to grow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, responsive layouts, meaningful link text, and reduced-motion support for animated background effects.
